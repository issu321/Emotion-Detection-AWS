Powerful Features

Discover everything EmoVoice can do to help you understand emotions through speech

Real-Time Emotion Detection

Upload any audio file and get instant emotion analysis. Our ANN model processes audio in under 1 second, delivering accurate predictions with confidence scores for all 7 emotion classes.

  • Instant processing under 1 second
  • Support for WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A, FLAC
  • Confidence scores for all predictions
  • Detailed probability distribution

Advanced Audio Feature Extraction

Our system extracts over 40 acoustic features using Librosa, including MFCCs, chroma features, mel spectrograms, spectral contrast, and temporal features for comprehensive analysis.

  • 40 MFCC coefficients
  • Chroma energy features
  • Mel-frequency spectrogram
  • Spectral contrast analysis
  • Zero-crossing rate & RMS energy

Interactive Dashboard

Visualize your emotion analysis results with beautiful, interactive charts. Track your emotional patterns over time with detailed statistics and trend analysis.

  • Real-time probability charts
  • Emotion distribution pie chart
  • Historical trend analysis
  • Confidence score tracking

ANN Architecture

Multi-layer neural network with optimized architecture for speech emotion recognition, trained on diverse datasets for robust performance.

Secure & Private

User authentication with encrypted passwords. All audio processing happens locally, ensuring your data remains private and secure.

Emotion History

Complete history of all your emotion analyses with timestamps, confidence scores, and downloadable reports for personal tracking.

Responsive Design

Access your dashboard from any device. Fully responsive interface optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile experiences.

Multiple Formats

Support for all major audio formats including WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A, and FLAC with automatic format detection and conversion.

High Performance

Optimized processing pipeline with efficient feature extraction and fast inference, handling files up to 16MB with ease.
