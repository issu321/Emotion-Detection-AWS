How It Works

Our end-to-end pipeline processes audio through several sophisticated stages:

1

Audio Collection

Users upload audio files (WAV, MP3, OGG, M4A, FLAC) through our intuitive web interface. The system accepts recordings up to 16MB in size.

2

Signal Preprocessing

Audio signals are normalized, resampled to a standard rate, and segmented into 3-second clips for consistent analysis.

3

Feature Extraction

We extract 40+ features including MFCCs, chroma features, mel spectrogram, spectral contrast, zero-crossing rate, and RMS energy using Librosa.

4

ANN Classification

The Artificial Neural Network processes extracted features through multiple hidden layers with ReLU activation and softmax output for 7 emotion classes.

5

Real-Time Dashboard

Results are displayed instantly with confidence scores, probability distributions, and stored in your personal history for trend analysis.

Emotion Classes

Our ANN model is trained to recognize seven fundamental human emotions from speech:

Neutral

Calm, balanced tone

Happy

Joyful, upbeat speech

Sad

Melancholic, low energy

Angry

Intense, harsh tone

Fearful

Anxious, trembling voice

Disgusted

Revulsion, aversion

Surprised

Astonishment, shock
